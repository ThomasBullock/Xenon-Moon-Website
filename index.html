Welcome to Xenon Moon

On a newly discovered frozen moon in the Gliese 667 system, humanity has set out to explore, mine and colonise an exciting new world. Your arrival on Xenon Moon comes just years after a number incidents revolving around the disappearance of your brother Thorsten Blake. All is not what it seems in the craters of Xenon Moon. Mysterious attacks from an unknown enemy now plague the local population. Follow your own path as you explore, mine, complete objectives, solve puzzles, upgrade your gamma quad, as well as engage in fierce combat. All while striving to find out what has happened to your brother, and to reveal the secrets of Xenon Moon.
